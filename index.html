About Minute Math:

Minute Math is a free to play, open-source math game, designed to help you sharpen your math skills in just a few minutes a day. I made this as a simple portfolio project inspired by games like Minute Cryptic and Wordle but with a mathematical twist. I believe that math is a skill that can be improved with practice, and Minute Math is here to help you do just that. As a student in computer science, math is a skill that is often used and needs consistent practice to hone your skills so i started this as a way to practise math myself. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do!

How to play:

Each day, Minute Math presents you with a new math problem to solve: short, focused, and designed to test your skills. The problems range from algebra to logic-based puzzles, and they’re meant to be solved in just a few minutes. Simply read the question, work it out on paper, and enter your answer in the input box. You’ll earn points for correct solutions and build a streak by solving daily problems consistently. No timers, no pressure! just one problem a day to keep your brain sharp. Come back tomorrow for a new challenge!

Math Help:

Need a quick refresher on some basic algebra concepts? Open Algebra is a free, open-source resource packed with explanations, examples, and step-by-step breakdowns. Use it to get targeted help with the kinds of problems you’ll see in Minute Math, from basic operations to more advanced algebra concepts.
