About Minute Math

Minute Math is a free daily math puzzle game featuring one new challenge every day, designed to be solved in just a few minutes.

What You'll Find

Topics include algebra, calculus, geometry, trigonometry, logarithms, probability, sequences, and other mathematical puzzles. Every problem has a single correct solution and encourages logical thinking over memorization.

Answer Formatting

Answers are entered using LaTeX-style formatting and render automatically as you type. The KB button can be used to insert supported mathematical functions and symbols.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make mathematics enjoyable through short, challenging daily problems that are accessible to students, hobbyists, and anyone who enjoys problem solving.

About the Creator

Minute Math was created by Luca Mazzotta as a personal project to combine mathematics, programming, and game design into a free educational experience.
