Frequently Asked Questions

Minute Math

What is Minute Math?

Minute Math is a daily math challenge game that presents one new math problem each day. The problems are short but can range in difficulty from basic algebra to more advanced logical puzzles.

Is there a time limit?

Nope! Despite the name, there's no timer. The name "Minute Math" refers to the idea that each problem should only take a few minutes to solve.

Do I need to make an account?

No account is required to play! However, if you do create an account later (coming soon), your score and streak can be saved across devices.

Can I play past problems?

Not yet, but we're working on a past problem archive. Stay tuned!

How is my score calculated?

You get one chance to solve each daily problem. Answering correctly adds to your score and your streak. Missing the problem ends your streak, but your score stays saved in your browser.

Where can I get help with the math?

We recommend checking out our References page for algebra laws and resources, or visiting OpenAlgebra.com.
